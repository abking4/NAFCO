What it truly takes to do what we do.

There’s a lot more to structural steel fabrication than designing, detailing, cutting, drilling, and welding. The true test of any fabricator is in its ability to deliver projects on time, within budget, and to spec—over and over and over again.

For 70 years, North Alabama Fabricating Company (NAFCO) has been doing just that. In the beginning, we specialized in structural detailing, but have since proved our mettle in engineering, fabrication and erection of structural steel of all kinds. Power plants, commercial buildings, hangars, material handling, or warehouses– NAFCO continues to deliver!

Services

Structural Steel Fabrication

From Federal projects and stadiums to warehouses and office buildings and everything in between. Large or small, local or nation-wide. NAFCO is your fabricator of choice for steel fabrication and erection projects. Envolved in countless successful projects our team is versed in long term envolvement in projects from start to completion. No need for second parties or intermediaries. We will complete the tast garantee.

Preconstruction and Design-Assist Services

Our team actively participates in budgeting and offers in-house design-assist services to streamline workflows, reduce costs, and accelerate project timelines. With robust in-house engineering capabilities based in our Birmingham office and our Chicago-based division, Wolf Point Engineers, we provide connection design, stair design, and full-scale engineering services to support your project from concept through completion.

Industrial Steel Fabrication

NAFCO is the industry leader in tubular gallery conveyors and provides heavy platework, structural steel, chutes, bins, hoppers, and silos across the United States and the world.

Structural Detailing Services

NAFCO’s in-house detailing operation began in 1953 and we’re still at it today. We use the latest modelling technology with both Tekla and SDS/2, along with decades of detailing experience to understand your project and provide best-in-class detailing services.

Sophisticated Coatings

NAFCO’s climate-controlled, sophisticated-endorsed facilities combined with years of experience enable us to expertly apply complex, multi-coat paint systems entirely in-house. We handle everything from blasting and surface preparation to the application of zinc, epoxy, polyurethane, polysiloxane, and other advanced coatings. This comprehensive in-shop approach ensures consistent quality, reduces reliance on fieldwork, and leads to significant time and cost savings. Additionally, as the industry increasingly shifts toward shop-applied intumescent fireproofing, NAFCO stands at the forefront—offering integrated detailing, fabrication, and coating services that streamline project schedules and meet stringent fireproofing requirements with precision and efficiency.
